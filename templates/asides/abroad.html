Abroad

Spain

Spring 2016

I studied abroad in Cordoba, Spain spring semester of 2016. It was an amazing experience. I lived with a Spanish family who only didn't speak any English, and two of my classes were taught in Spanish. The history of Cordoba is pretty amazing - in fact, my backyard had a 600 year old Muslim wall, and a 2,000 year old Roman column in it.


Europe

Summer 2016

I decided to stay in Europe that summer. I traveled up through Portugal, then through northern Spain, then I flew to Sweden to visit a friend. The two of us flew to Iceland where we rented a car and camped for 8 days. From there I flew to Prague and worked my way south through Budapest, Belgrade, Kosovo, until I hit the Greek islands.
